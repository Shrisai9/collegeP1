About Us

Amazon.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company based in Seattle, Washington, which focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. It was founded by Jeff Bezos on July 5, 1994.

Our Mission

To be Earth's most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they want to buy online, and endeavors to offer its customers the lowest possible prices.

Our History

Amazon started as an online bookstore, but soon diversified, selling DVDs, CDs, MP3 downloads, software, video games, electronics, apparel, furniture, food, toys, and jewelry. Today, Amazon is the world's largest online retailer, providing services such as cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and more.

Our Team

Our team is made up of talented individuals from diverse backgrounds and experiences. We are united by a passion for delivering exceptional customer service and creating innovative solutions that make shopping easier and more enjoyable for our customers.

Our Values

We are guided by four core values: customer obsession, ownership, invent and simplify, and are right a lot. These values shape our culture and guide our decisions.
